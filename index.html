Pure Water Technology

We use the latest in window cleaning technology - a pure water system delivered through extendable poles. This method allows us to thoroughly clean your glass, frames, and sills from the safety of the ground. Each window is carefully scrubbed and rinsed to ensure a spotless, streak-free shine.

Our water is purified through a multi-stage filtration process, removing all minerals and impurities. This ensures a crystal-clear finish every time, with no need for detergents or harsh chemicals.

The Benefits of Pure Water Window Cleaning:

  • Safe and Private: Most windows can be cleaned safely from ground level – no ladders, no disturbance.
  • Self-Sufficient: We carry our own purified water supply – there’s no need to use your tap or hose.
  • Hard-to-Reach? No Problem: Our high-reach poles and specialized brush heads make it easy to clean upper-storey, skylight, and Velux windows.
  • Eco-Friendly: We avoid using soaps or chemicals unless absolutely necessary, making our service gentle on the environment.

Please note - Your windows will be left wet after cleaning, this is normal. As the pure water dries, it leaves behind a sparkling, streak-free finish.
